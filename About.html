ABOUT

BeWell is a wellbeing startup that was established in 2020. It's aim is to give counseling and help to those with mental health issues and build devices in maintaining mental wellbeing such as stress spinners. It also offers therapy and special wellbeing massages.

This service makes sure that our nation's populations are all mentally healthy and tried to educate people about mental health issues and highlight their importance. The primal goal is just to make Zimbabwe a happier nation altogether.
